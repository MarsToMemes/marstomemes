About MarsToMeme

MarsToMeme is a memecoin inspired by the spirit of exploration and humor of Elon Musk, created for all dreamers who aim to conquer Mars for the memes! Join the mission and become a "Martian Meme Explorer" as you journey towards the red planet with Doge as your guide.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 154,600,000 MTM
  • Blockchain: Solana (SPL Token)
  • Symbol: MTM

Features

  • Progression to Mars: Advance through levels like "Beginner Earthling," "Mars Settler," and "Meme Martian," collecting unique badges and NFTs to mark your journey.
  • Exclusive "Mars Souvenirs" NFTs: Collect special NFTs of Doge and other memes, capturing iconic moments of the Martian journey.
